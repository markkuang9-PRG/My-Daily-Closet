# Governance

## Project Owner

My Daily Closet is maintained under the direction of Piedra Roja Group, Inc.

The project owner retains final authority over:

- roadmap and product direction;
- acceptance or rejection of contributions;
- release decisions;
- trademark use;
- licensing strategy, including commercial licensing and relicensing where permitted by repository terms and contributor agreements;
- business transactions involving the project or its assets.

## Maintainers

Maintainers are designated by the project owner.

Maintainers may:

- review and merge pull requests;
- close or label issues;
- moderate community participation;
- request changes before accepting contributions.

## Contributors

Contributors are welcome to propose code, documentation, designs, bug reports, and feature ideas, but contribution does not create governance, ownership, or veto rights over project direction.

## Decision Making

The project aims to work in public where practical, but the project owner may make private decisions regarding legal, security, licensing, branding, commercial partnerships, or acquisition-related matters.
